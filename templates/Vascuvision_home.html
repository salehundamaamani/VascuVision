Welcome to Vascuvision

Our mission is to transform the way cardiovascular health is assessed and managed, leveraging advanced technologies to provide accurate, efficient, and comprehensive solutions.

Our Vision and Mission

Revolutionize Cardiac Imaging Analysis

We strive to revolutionize cardiac imaging analysis by leveraging advanced AI and deep learning techniques to provide accurate, efficient, and comprehensive solutions.

Enhance Diagnostic Accuracy

Our mission is to enhance diagnostic accuracy in cardiovascular health assessments by integrating multi-modal data from MRI, CT, and SPECT-MPI scans.

Reduce Interpretation Time

We aim to reduce interpretation time for cardiac imaging analysis, enabling clinicians to make timely and informed decisions in managing cardiovascular diseases.

Improve Patient Outcomes

Our ultimate goal is to improve patient outcomes in managing cardiovascular diseases through innovative AI-driven solutions that empower clinicians and improve healthcare delivery.

About Us

VascuVision is a collaborative project led by researchers from the University of Windsor, Canada. Our team is dedicated to advancing the field of cardiac imaging analysis through cutting-edge research, technology development, and clinical collaboration.

By combining expertise in AI, deep learning, and cardiology,we aim to address the challenges and opportunities in cardiac diagnostics and make a meaningful impact on patient care.
